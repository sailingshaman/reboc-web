Your neutral intermediary in IT

Reboc B.V. was founded in The Hague with a clear mission: to connect exceptional freelance IT professionals with forward-thinking enterprises — and take care of everything in between.

As a neutral intermediary, we facilitate the full cycle: from project intake to contracting and invoicing. Our clients get access to deep expertise in project management, cloud infrastructure, data visualisation, and software development — without the overhead.

We ensure clarity, continuity, and compliance for both clients and freelancers, so everyone can focus on what they do best: delivering results.
